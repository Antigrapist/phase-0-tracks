Fun Hedgehog Facts

Here's some cool info about nature's coolest little animal

  • Hedgehogs are nocturnal (i.e. they are mostly active at night)
  • They have poor vision and rely on their hearing and scent
  • Hedgehogs love to eat insects
Popular Hedgehog names:
  1. Sonic
  2. Spike
  3. Prickles
